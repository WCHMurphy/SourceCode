About Murphy Game

Murphy is a web and mobile game application that challenges players to work together to solve problems based on clues provided on cards. Each team is given 28 cards with clues, and the team that solves the problem first wins. Murphy is designed to promote teamwork and problem-solving skills, while providing an entertaining and engaging experience for players of all ages.
